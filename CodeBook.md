## Identification Fields

* `subject` - The participant ("subject") ID. Values: 1-30.
* `activity` - The label of the activity performed when the corresponding measurements were taken:
1. `WALKING`
2. `WALKING_UPSTAIRS`
3. `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS`
4. `SITTING`
5. `STANDING`
6. `LAYING`
* `run`: test or train.
